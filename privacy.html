Privacy Policy

This demo stores your form entries on your own device using localStorage. We do not transmit your data to any server.

  • Registration: name, email, and phone are saved locally.
  • Consultation chat: messages are saved locally and can be cleared by clearing your browser data.
  • Diagnosis: selected symptoms and notes may be stored locally for your history.
  • Appointments: bookings are saved locally so they show on the Home page.

Because there is no cloud storage, if you switch devices or clear site data you will lose your records. For a production site, use a secure backend, HTTPS, and healthcare‑grade data protections.
